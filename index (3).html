About Me

Hi, I'm Megan! I'm a student at Brigham Young University studying Accounting. I'm a returned missionary from Ribeirão Preto, Brazil where I served for 18 months and became fluent in Portuguese.

I'm passionate about running, having competed in cross country and track all four years at Upper Arlington High School. I bring the same discipline from the track to everything I do — whether it's coursework, volunteer work, or a new challenge.
