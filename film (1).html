What I Look For

Watching elite runners isn't just entertainment — it's a masterclass in form, pacing, and mental toughness. Here's what I study:

Race Tactics

How runners position themselves, when they make their move, and how they respond to competitors — every race is a chess match.

Running Form

Foot strike, arm swing, cadence — elite form translates directly to efficiency and injury prevention.

Mental Toughness

The moment a race gets hard, you see who's done the mental work. Watching how athletes push through pain is deeply motivating.
