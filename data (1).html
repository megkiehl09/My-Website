Why Data Matters in Running

Data has transformed how athletes train and compete. From split times to VO₂ max, the numbers reveal patterns invisible to the naked eye. As an accounting student, I'm naturally drawn to the analytical side of sport — what the data says and, just as importantly, what it doesn't.

Visualizations like this help coaches and athletes identify strengths and weaknesses in ways pure intuition can't match.
